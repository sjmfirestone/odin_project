Creamy Vegan Corn Chowder

I was experimenting with different menu ideas when I became a vegan and came across this great low-fat comfort food: creamy vegan corn chowder recipe. It's great as a meal starter or as the main course.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 cup celery, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 2 cube vegetable bouillon
  • 2 cups Corn
  • 2 cups soy milk
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 1 tablespoon ultra-vegan flour
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and celery; cook until just slightly golden. Stir in carrots and garlic; cook until garlic is slightly golden.
  2. Meanwhile, bring water to a boil in a pot over high heat.
  3. Add bouillon cubes, reduce heat to medium, and stir until cubes are dissolved.
  4. Add corn and onion mixture; cook until vegetables are tender, adding water, if necessary.
  5. Reduce heat to low, pour in 1 cup soy milk, stir well, then stir in remaining 1 cup soy milk.
  6. Quickly whisk in flour; stir in parsley, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
  7. Cook until chowder thickens, stirring constantly, 15 to 20 minutes.
