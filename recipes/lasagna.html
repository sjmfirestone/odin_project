Vegan Lasagna I

Ingredients

  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups chopped onion
  • 3 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 4 (14.5 ounce) cans stewed tomatos
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup vegan mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook lasagna noodles until al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, and saut√© until onion is translucent.
  4. Stir in stewed tomatoes, mushrooms, and zucchini. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, layer noodles, vegetable mixture, and vegan mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used.
  6. Cover with aluminum foil and bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
  8. Serve with a side salad and garlic bread for a complete meal.
  9. Enjoy your delicious vegan lasagna!
      10. Note: You can also add other vegetables like spinach, bell peppers, or eggplant to the lasagna for added flavor and nutrition.
